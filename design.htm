Sahara

The Sahara Desert, often referred to as the "Great Desert," stands as the largest hot desert on Earth, spanning across North Africa and encompassing parts of Algeria, Chad, Egypt, Libya, Mali, Mauritania, Morocco, Niger, Sudan, and Tunisia. Characterized by its vast expanses of undulating sand dunes, rocky plateaus, and arid landscapes, the Sahara covers an astonishing area of approximately 9.2 million square kilometers.
